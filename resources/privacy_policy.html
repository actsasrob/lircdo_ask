  • During the Skill setup the IP address of your server-side computer and lircdo application port number will be requested. The Skill will perform a DNS name lookup to determine the fully qualified domain name (FQDN) of your server-side computer. The FQDN and port number will be used for future communication between the Skill and your lircdo server-side computer/application. If the FQDN does not resolve to a static IP you must provide a dynamic DNS mechansim to update the FQDN with the dynamic IP for your internet service.
